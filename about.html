Who we are:

There are a great number of degree and engineering colleges imparting education to lakhs of students – every year, based on the curriculum designed by the universities they are affiliated to. Unfortunately, more often than not, the syllabi covered during the entire course of study is oriented more towards academics than towards the industry. This creates a wide gulf between what the students are trained-in and what the industry demands. Hence, the students find themselves wanting – in the very skills they need, to find employment.

On the other hand, to compound this problem further, the prospective employers prefer candidates with experience, which is not possible to get unless they find employment in the first place. We at Train2Pro, facilitate the employment process of potential candidates by training them in exactly those skills that the prospective employers seek and make them job-ready.

With new technologies emerging very frequently, the skills one possesses tend to become obsolete soon. We keep a constant watch on what skill-sets the employers look for, in the candidates and connect with those resource persons who are proficient in imparting those skill-sets and make them available at our centre to train the candidates. In addition we have a tie-up with a lot of organizations to provide them with trained man-power. This places us – Train2Pro, at a pedestal from where we can facilitate the candidates who come to us, to get employed – soon after their training.

Mission and Vision

Specializations abound in the entire spectrum of education – be it in engineering, management, fundamental sciences, arts, commerce or humanities. In each of these domains there are many streams. While those students who are proficient in their subjects generally land in a job quickly, we do reconcile to the fact that a lot of average students don’t get employed and are forced to move on to a different domain or field. Hence it is our endeavour to harness the potential in them and train them in the very skills sought after by the prospective employers, leading to their employment.In short, we groom them intensely – like in a finishing school.

    Recruitment Process Outsourcing and Methodology

    We provide recruitment services to corporate to help them select and onboard the right and best skilled candidates. We render our services to colleges in helping them to conduct campus drives. We are experts in providing trained freshers in various technologies and skill sets as the technology and corporate requirements are changing time to time. We are specialists in Hire, Train and Deploy Modules as per the client requirements.
    Real time case studies are given to the trainees which helps them to take on the corporate challenges more positively and efficiently. They get an opportunity to interact with consultants and trainers having profound experience in the field of Human Resource, Corporate Accounts, Finance and different ERPs among others. By this, we cater to the needs of the corporates, by providing them with trained-freshers who otherwise find it difficult to get an opportunity to work in the corporate world. We do this pan-India, including rural areas, where candidates need to be trained more intensely.

    To sum it up, the simple objective of Train2Pro Training And Consulting Services LLP, is to find a job for the multitude of average students, who don't get placed in their campus interviews and then run from pillar to post to find a job.

    We wish to hand-hold them and lessen their anxiety and frustration until they get placed eventually.
